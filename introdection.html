The list tag in HTML is used to organize and structure content into a list format, making it easier to read and understand.There are three main types of list tags:
    1. Ordered List (OL): 
        Used for lists with a specific order or sequence, such as steps, rankings, or instructions.
    2. Unordered List (UL): 
        Used for lists without a specific order or sequence, such as items, features, or options.
    3. Definition List (DL): 
        Used for lists with terms and definitions, such as glossaries or dictionaries.
